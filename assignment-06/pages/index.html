Feed Your Followers!


WARNING:

If a Follower goes several days without eating, they will gain the Starving status. The Starvation meter over their head will slowly fill; if it turns completely red, the Follower dies immediately. Starving Followers have the status removed as soon as they eat a meal of any kind.

Hungry Followers will stop what they are doing and eat a meal if one is available. Meals are given to the hungriest Followers first, and the Flock is smart enough that multiple Followers won't try to eat the same meal. The exception to this is if a Follower has requested a specific meal as part of a quest; in these cases, that Follower skips the line and eats the first plate of the requested meal that is cooked.

Some helpful ingridents when making meals for your followers include:

Beetroot
Found in Silk Cradle or grown on Farm Plots.
Berries
Found in Darkwood or grown on Farm Plots.
Bone
Dropped by enemies; smash their skeletons to collect.
Grass
Found by cutting tall grass while on crusade.
Morsel
Catch spiders in the village at night or trap birds with an upgraded Scarecrow.
Follower Meat
Can be harvested from dead Followers. Also received after performing a Sacrifice.

Quality meals are expensive to make, requiring lots of grinding for the best ingredients. However, they have the power to make your Followers give you gifts and can even bring Dissenters back into the fold!

Some of the most helpful recipes are:

  • Delicious Fish Feast
  • Tasty Fish Meal
  • Mighty Meat Feast
  • Hearty Meat Broth
  • Magnificent Mixed Meal
  • Modest Mixed Meal
